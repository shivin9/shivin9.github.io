Shivin Srivastava

Assistant Professor (EEE)

BITS Pilani, Goa Campus

I am an Assistant Professor at the EEE department at BITS Pilani, Goa campus. My research interested is in AI enabled semiconductor manufacturing. Previously, I was a Ph.D. student at the National University of Singapore in School of Computing where I worked on Subtype-Aware Risk Modeling in Clinical (ICU) contexts using deep representation learning techniques with Prof. Vaibhav Rajan. Previously, I have worked on Anomaly Detection, Natural Language Processing and Association Rule Mining. I have also built an efficient library for solving PDEs with JuliaLabs in MIT.
